• Type a or b to toogle display region
  • Use arrow ← ↑ → ↓ to edit selected region (mouse doesn't work yet)
  • Shift + ← or → to skew region
  • Shift + ↑ or ↓ to change preprocessing contrast
  • Ctrl + ← or → to adjust space between digits
  • Number 1 - 9 to choose number of digits
  • t to toogle show preprocessed region
